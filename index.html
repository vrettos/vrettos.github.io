About Me

Hello and welcome to my page! I'm coming from Athens, Greece, and I'm currently pursuing a PhD in EECS with a designated emphasis in Statistics at the UC Berkeley,
where I'm being advised by Satish Rao. I hold a MSc in EECS from the UC Berkeley, as well as a Diploma in ECE from the National Technical Univesity of Athens.

Research

Broadly speaking my research interests lie in the areas of probability, statistics, information theory, learning theory, stochastic control and optimization.
In particularly, I have researched topics about concentration inequalities for Markov chains, and multi-armed bandit problems.
