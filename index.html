About Me

Hello and welcome to my page! I'm coming from Athens, Greece, and I'm currently pursuing a PhD in EECS with a designated emphasis in Statistics at the UC Berkeley,
where I'm being advised by Satish Rao. I hold a MSc in EECS from the UC Berkeley, as well as a Diploma in ECE from the National Technical Univesity of Athens where
