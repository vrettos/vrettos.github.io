About Me

Hello and welcome to my page! I'm coming from Athens, Greece, and I'm currently a quantitative researcher at The Voleon Group, where I conduct machine learning research in order to forecast the stock markets. Before that I obtained a PhD in EECS with a designated emphasis in Statistics from the UC Berkeley,
where I was advised by Satish Rao. I hold a MSc in EECS from the UC Berkeley, as well as a Diploma in ECE from the National Technical Univesity of Athens where
I was advised by Nikolaos Papaspyrou.

Research

My research interests lie in the following areas:

  • probability

  • statistics

  • information theory

  • statistical learning theory

  • machine learning

  • stochastic control

  • optimization

In particular, I have researched:

  • concentration inequalities

  • Markov processes

  • multi-armed bandit problems

  • optimal transport
